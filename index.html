The single input/output parcel distributor

ASD machine is a revolutionary parcel dispenser. The purpose of this machine is to provide delivery companies a new way to tackle the "last mile" problem. The distributor is totally automated from the introduction of the parcel to the restition of it. Moreover, the system was tought in order to optimize internal storage space needed and to minimize the outside size in order to save a large visible commercial space.

At Your Service

Parcel Storage

The machine stores packages of maximum 25kg with a dimension of max 40cm x 40cm x 60cm.

100% Secured Machine

Storage place is not accessible from anyone : customer as postman.

Identification Technology

Parcels and customers are identified thanks to advanced recognition technologies.

Payement Terminal

The paying process is totally secured and managed by the machine.
